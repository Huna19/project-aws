### Weekly outcomes:

The first week was mostly about "getting familiar" — learning foundational concepts: what cloud is, AWS benefits, global infrastructure, shared responsibility model, EC2, instance types, pricing, auto scaling, load balancing. Many concepts were still unclear, but at least I had a direction to continue.

The most time-consuming part was creating the AWS account — had to try **4 different VISA cards** before one worked. After that, I enabled billing alerts, set up a budget, and completed tasks to receive **100 USD**. By the end of the week, I successfully launched an **EC2 Ubuntu** instance, installed **Node.js**, and ran a backend using **PM2** — the first time seeing an app actually running on the cloud, not just in theory.

Saturday and Sunday I went back home but still took time to review week 1 content to keep the foundational knowledge fresh.
