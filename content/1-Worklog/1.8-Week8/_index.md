### Week 8 Achievements:

This week I combined technical work with valuable career orientation learning. On the technical side: researched **event-driven architecture** and designed asynchronous processing flow using **Amazon SQS** + **SNS** for the ticket booking project — when many people book simultaneously, payment requests are pushed to **SQS queue** for workers to process, then **SNS** sends confirmation emails + e-tickets to customers.

The biggest highlight was **attending the FCAJ Meetup on 06/13** — hearing about the real-world landscape of **DevOps**, **Data Analytics** careers at multinational corporations (MNC) and development roadmaps with the AWS community. I consolidated **5 career development stages** (from Follower to System Thinker) and learned about rigorous standards in digital supply chains like **ISO 27001**, **SOC 2**.

The most impressive part was learning global-standard corporate culture: "**No-Blame**", "**Caring & Inclusive**" alongside the "**Đúng việc**" philosophy (Being human, Being professional, Being a citizen) — helping shape a professional working mindset, not just being technically skilled but also having the right mindset.
