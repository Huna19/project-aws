### Weekly outcomes:

This week marks a milestone — closing out the theory phase and preparing to move into real hands-on project work. The two most important topics were Migration (6R strategies, AWS CAF, Snow Family) and especially the **AWS Well-Architected Framework**.

Understanding the 6 pillars isn't just about memorizing names — it's about understanding why each pillar exists and what real problem it solves. For example: **Cost Optimization** isn't "use as few services as possible" — it's "use the right service at the right time." **Reliability** isn't just high uptime — it's also the ability to self-recover when failures happen.

The **AWS Pricing Calculator** practice was more practical than expected — estimating costs for an EC2 + RDS + S3 architecture made it clear which factors drive the biggest portion of a monthly bill. This is a skill worth having before designing any system.
