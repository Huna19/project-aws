### Weekly outcomes:

This was a milestone week — covering Migration (Snow Family, AWS CAF) and most importantly the **AWS Well-Architected Framework**. Understanding the 6 design pillars (Security, Reliability, Performance, Cost Optimization, Operational Excellence, Sustainability) is the foundation for designing architectures that are not just "working" but actually built right.

The **AWS Pricing Calculator** practice was also a practical eye-opener — knowing how to estimate costs before deploying is a real-world skill most beginners overlook. By the end of the week, all knowledge from week 1 onward was reviewed and consolidated — ready to move into real project work.
